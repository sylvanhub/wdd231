Brief History of the Pearl's Touch Unisex Salon

The salon was founded in 2016 with the initial aim of providing hair services for the students in the Federal College of Agriculture and the College of Animal Health and Production Technology. The salon specializes in various services such as barbering, dreadlocks, Ghana weaving and braiding for women, nails and eyelashes fixing, hair treatment and dread nursing, hair coloring, and affordable training programs for students. The website is designed to be user-friendly, facilitating interaction between the salon owner and clients. Visitors can find answers to questions about hair care, information about the salon location, book services, and choose from different apprentices for their hair services.
