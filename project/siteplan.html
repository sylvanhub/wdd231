PEARL'S TOURCH UNISEX SALON

Website Planning Document

Site Name

This name was chosen because it represents a stylish and inclusive salon that caters to all genders, providing a variety of beauty and grooming services.

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a platform for PEARL'S TOURCH UNISEX SALON, offering information about services, booking appointments, showcasing hairstyles, and providing beauty tips and product recommendations.

Visitor Scenarios

Scenario 1: What types of services do you offer for men and women?

Scenario 2: How can I book an appointment for a haircut?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #1a73e8 (Used for headings, navigation bar, and accent elements)

Secondary Color: #f0f4f8 (Used for background and sections)

Typography

Font 1: Arial, sans-serif (Used for body text)

Font 2: Georgia, serif (Used for headings)
